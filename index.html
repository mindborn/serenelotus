About us

We provide comprehensive engineering and CAD services for the AEC industry, combining BIM-driven collaboration with standards-compliant delivery to help teams design, coordinate, and build with confidence.

Approach

From survey data and existing topography to concept design and feasibility analysis, our workflows prioritize accuracy, reproducibility, and clear documentation for each project phase.

Delivery

We produce GFC-ready drawings, cut/fill analyses, profiles, cross-sections, and automated plan sets—reducing iteration cycles and improving coordination across disciplines.

Vision

Be the trusted engineering partner enabling sustainable, resilient infrastructure through data-driven design, advanced modelling, and collaborative delivery.

Mission

Empower clients with precise engineering, BIM-centred coordination, and scalable workflows that align with standards while meeting real-world constraints on time and cost.

Services

End-to-end infrastructure engineering across planning, design, modelling, analysis, and documentation.

Earthwork & site grading

Feature lines, grading tools, cut/fill, quantity estimates, lot grading, and parcel creation.

Roadway engineering

Alignment design, corridors, horizontal/vertical geometry, cross-sections, culverts, and intersections.

Traffic engineering

Vehicle tracking, Autoturn simulations, vertical/horizontal clearance checks and route feasibility.

Public health engineering

Water, sewer, storm networks; L-sections; utility crossings and integrated township layouts.

Land surveying

ALTA surveys, lot/boundary topography, drone mapping and UAV-based terrain modelling.

CAD & BIM delivery

Drafting, drawing sets, model coordination, BIM-based collaboration, and GFC documentation.
